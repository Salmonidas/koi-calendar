Subscribe to Google Calendars for your favourite KOI teams and follow every match directly in your Google Calendar.

**👉 [koi-calendar-coral.vercel.app](https://koi-calendar-coral.vercel.app/)**

---

## What is this?

A free web app where you can generate a personalised Google Calendar with the matches of the KOI teams you choose.
Just pick the teams you want to follow, the language and the colour, and you'll get a direct link to add the calendar to your Google account in one click.

Matches are updated automatically, so your calendar is always up to date without you having to do anything.
